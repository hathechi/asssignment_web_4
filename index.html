WEB EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTE is an

Innovative Educational

Group of India.

The Web Educational Institute (WEI) has consistently displayed full awareness of its social responsibilities through the Web Educational Society (WES), a 'not for profit' Trust. The Group believes that Education is the cornerstone to economic development and which alone can assist build India into a developed nation. WEI Committed to delivering excellence from diploma level education to higher level education, postdoctoral, vocational training and research.

A personalized approach to Learning is to puts our students at the heart of everything we do.

Studies and research on the life WEI believes in spreading education among educationally backward classes and communities were the other commitment. In consonance with these expectations, WEI is promoting education in such fields as science, arts, management, information technology, industrial technology, environment, planning and architecture. Carefully nurtured by committed academia, the WEI has carved a niche for itself in the academic world.

WEI have developed a flexible learning structure, so that students can build a program that suits their individual needs. From vocational learning to postdoctoral studies, students have more options. WEI has earned a big reputation for high-impact research. WEI have Industry Based Learning Centers that respond to industry and government priorities, and society needs.

WEI driven to find innovative research solutions to real world problems.

At WEI we seek solution for real problems. So we collaborate with industry to ensure our graduates are work ready, and able to meet the challenge of industry needs.

At WEI we're committed to genuine sustainability. Our Social and built environment are designed with the most up-to-date sustain ability principles.

Sustainability is embedded in our teaching, learning and research, so that we are working on practical solutions for the 21st Century. We know our future depends on it.

Our Vision

The vision of the Web Educational Institute is to provide world class quality education at affordable cost so that even those students who are living under the poverty line can opt for higher education and compete with the general society. We believe in giving best education to all with innovative & latest curriculum based on Indian & International standards through distance learning education / correspondence programs.

Our Mission

Our aim is to be a Learning Organization which will use measuring and monitoring tools to continually enhance the effectiveness of its students, thereby to differentiate itself as an Academy that is continually renewing and transforming itself to meet the changing needs of its students. Our objective is to deliver the highest quality distance education programs through innovative forms of flexible distance learning mode, leveraging the latest technology platform. WEI fits student’s calendar, harmonizes with their career & compliments student’s personal endeavors.
